$a=9.8m/s^2$

$x=?$

$y=?$

$x=vt$

$x = a \cos\theta$

$y = a \sin\theta$
## Solution

$v_{0_x} = 21.45m/s$

$v_{0_y} = 28m/s \cdot \sin 40\degree$

$v_{0_y} = 18.00m/s$

$y = 18.00m/s \cdot 2s - \frac{1}{2}9.8m/s^2 \cdot (2s)^2$

$y = 16.4m$

$x = 21.45m/s * 2s$

$x = 42.9m$

> [!info] Final Answer
